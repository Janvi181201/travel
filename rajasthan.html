Royal Rajasthan Journey

Jaipur • Jodhpur • Udaipur – The Land of Kings Awaits

Tour Overview

Discover the regal heritage of Rajasthan in this 6-day cultural tour through Jaipur, Jodhpur, and Udaipur. Marvel at royal forts, palaces, traditional arts, and desert landscapes that define India's majestic past.

Detailed Itinerary – 6 Days of Royalty

  • Day 1: Arrival in Jaipur – Visit City Palace & Hawa Mahal
  • Day 2: Amber Fort & local shopping in Jaipur
  • Day 3: Travel to Jodhpur – Mehrangarh Fort, local cuisine
  • Day 4: Explore Jaswant Thada & blue city walk
  • Day 5: Travel to Udaipur – City Palace & Lake Pichola boat ride
  • Day 6: Visit Jagdish Temple, return journey
