Golden Triangle Expedition

Delhi • Agra • Jaipur – Explore India’s Cultural Crown

Tour Overview

The Golden Triangle Tour is a classic introduction to India’s rich history, royal palaces, majestic forts, and vibrant bazaars. This 5-day journey covers Delhi, Agra, and Jaipur — forming a triangle on the map — and showcases North India's cultural brilliance.

Detailed Itinerary – 5 Days of Discovery

  • Day 1: Arrival in Delhi – Explore Lotus Temple, India Gate
  • Day 2: Delhi sightseeing – Red Fort, Qutub Minar, local markets
  • Day 3: Travel to Agra – Visit Taj Mahal & Agra Fort
  • Day 4: Drive to Jaipur – City Palace, Hawa Mahal
  • Day 5: Visit Amber Fort, return to Delhi, departure
