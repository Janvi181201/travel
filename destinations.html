Explore India by Region

North India

North India offers a rich tapestry of culture, heritage, and adventure. From the snow-clad Himalayas of Himachal Pradesh and Uttarakhand to the sacred ghats of Varanasi, this region is the spiritual heart of India. Marvel at the Mughal glory of the Taj Mahal, explore the Pink City of Jaipur, or trek through the valleys of Kashmir. It’s the perfect blend of history, faith, and natural beauty.

  • Top States: Rajasthan, Uttar Pradesh, Uttarakhand, Himachal Pradesh
  • Top Attractions: Taj Mahal, Golden Temple, Himalayan Treks, Rishikesh, Delhi monuments
  • Best Time: October to March

South India

South India is a lush, tranquil haven known for its palm-lined beaches, spice plantations, and majestic temples. Kerala's backwaters, Tamil Nadu’s Dravidian temples, Karnataka’s royal architecture, and Andhra’s beaches make it culturally and naturally rich. Ayurveda, classical dance, and warm hospitality await travelers seeking peace and tradition.

  • Top States: Kerala, Tamil Nadu, Karnataka, Andhra Pradesh
  • Top Attractions: Munnar, Hampi, Meenakshi Temple, Backwaters of Alleppey, Pondicherry
  • Best Time: November to February

West India

West India is colorful, royal, and bustling. From the palaces and deserts of Rajasthan to the white salt plains of Kutch, this region is full of heritage and celebration. Don’t miss Maharashtra’s caves, Gujarat’s handicrafts, or Goa’s Portuguese charm. This region is perfect for history buffs, art lovers, and beach seekers.

  • Top States: Rajasthan, Gujarat, Maharashtra, Goa
  • Top Attractions: Udaipur, Ajanta & Ellora Caves, Rann of Kutch, Mumbai, Goa beaches
  • Best Time: October to March

East India

East India is culturally deep and naturally diverse. Visit the ancient temples of Odisha, the colonial charm of Kolkata, or the forests of Jharkhand. With tribal art, classical dance forms, and spiritual centers, it offers a peaceful and insightful journey into India’s soul.

  • Top States: West Bengal, Odisha, Bihar, Jharkhand
  • Top Attractions: Konark Sun Temple, Howrah Bridge, Bodh Gaya, Chilika Lake
  • Best Time: November to February

Northeast India

A land of misty hills, vibrant tribes, and undiscovered wonders, Northeast India is a paradise for nature lovers and cultural explorers. With its pristine landscapes and rich biodiversity, you’ll find stunning waterfalls in Meghalaya, monasteries in Arunachal Pradesh, and tea gardens in Assam. It remains one of India’s best-kept secrets.

  • Top States: Assam, Meghalaya, Sikkim, Nagaland, Arunachal Pradesh
  • Top Attractions: Kaziranga National Park, Tawang Monastery, Cherrapunji, Loktak Lake
  • Best Time: October to May

Travel Tips for India

Pack Smart

Weather can vary greatly between regions. Carry layers, sunscreen, and sturdy footwear.

Respect Culture

India is culturally rich. Dress modestly in temples and respect local traditions.

Stay Hydrated

Use bottled or filtered water. Carry a reusable bottle with you during tours.
