Mystic Ladakh Adventure

Leh • Nubra Valley • Pangong Lake • Khardung La

Tour Overview

Embark on an unforgettable 6-day journey through the land of high passes – Ladakh. Explore Buddhist monasteries, crystal-clear lakes, scenic valleys, and the magnetic culture of the Himalayas.

Detailed Itinerary – 6 Days in Ladakh

  • Day 1: Arrival in Leh – Acclimatization, Leh Market visit
  • Day 2: Leh sightseeing – Shanti Stupa, Hall of Fame, Magnetic Hill
  • Day 3: Drive to Nubra Valley via Khardung La – Camel Safari at Hunder
  • Day 4: Nubra to Pangong Lake – Overnight by the lake
  • Day 5: Pangong to Leh – En route Chang La Pass
  • Day 6: Departure from Leh

What’s Included

  • 5 Nights Stay (Leh, Nubra, Pangong)
  • Daily Breakfast & Dinner
  • All Transfers by Private SUV
  • Inner Line Permits, Safari & Entry Fees
